Grisha Trilogy

The "Shadow and Bone" trilogy, written by Leigh Bardugo, is set in the Grishaverse, a world where magic and science intertwine. The story follows Alina Starkov, a young orphan who discovers she has a rare and powerful ability to summon light. This power makes her a target for both the Ravkan government and the Darkling, a powerful Grisha who can control shadows.

Shadow and Bone

Alina discovers her power and is taken to the royal court to train with the Grisha, an elite group of magical soldiers. She struggles to control her abilities and navigate court politics.

Siege and Storm

Alina seeks allies to help her fight the Darkling, who has gained new and terrifying powers. She must balance her growing power with her desire for a normal life and her feelings for her childhood friend, Mal.

Ruin and Rising

Alina and her allies make a final stand against the Darkling and his forces. Alina must confront her own fears and make sacrifices to save Ravka and the people she loves.
